Open Non-commercial Animal Identification Scheme (ONAIS)

The ONAIS system is used to register and identify animals who are strictly not held for commercial purposes.

A Universally Unique Identifier (UUID) is created upon registration of an animal. It is permanently resolvable and online-retrievable.

Scheme: "Domain" / "Suffix"
Sample: pet4.eu / abc

Register an animal

Send an email to register your animal:
register@pet4.eu

It is a strict requirement that the animal is not held for commercial purposes.
You will be asked to supply details about the animal like name, photo, description, residency, and human contact details.
Afterwards you will be supplied with a UUID which the animal should be tagged with in a safe and appropriate way.
Registration may be rejected by the service owner for any reason, esp. obviously inappropriate living conditions of the animal.

Identify an animal

Just enter the UUID as a URL into an internet browser, e.g. pet4.eu/abc
It will get resolved to https://pet4.eu/abc and details about the registered animal will be shown.
